Cat Food Guide

Types of Cat Food

Choosing the right food for your cat is essential for their health and happiness. Here are the main types of cat food available:

  • Dry Food: Convenient and cost-effective, dry food helps maintain dental health.
  • Wet Food: High in moisture, great for hydration and often more palatable for picky eaters.
  • Raw Food: Mimics a cat's natural diet, but requires careful handling to avoid contamination.
  • Prescription Food: Specially formulated for cats with specific health conditions.
